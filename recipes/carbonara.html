Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Recipe for traditional italian spaghetti carbonara

You can substitute guanciale for pancetta or bacon

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Egg yolks
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the Guanciale on a pan
  2. Mix the cheese and egg yolks
  3. Boil the spaghetti. The guanciale is salty, so don't add too much salt to the water
  4. Save a bit of the water from the spaghetti
  5. Add spaghetti to the guanciale and mix it with the egg and cheese mix. Make sure the heat isn't too high, so the egg doesn't scramble
  6. Add spaghetti water to make the dish creamy
  7. serve with extra cheese and freshly ground black pepper
