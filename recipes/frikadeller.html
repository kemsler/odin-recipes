Frikadeller

Description

This is a recipe for the traditional danish dish Frikadeller

You can serve these delicious meatballs with potatoes and gravy, or just with spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Ground veal and pork mix
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Aromat
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix all the Ingredients excl. butter
  2. Form the mixture into medium sized meatballs
  3. Fry for 10 minutes on each side in butter
  4. Make sure the meatballs are cooked through
  5. Serve the meatballs
