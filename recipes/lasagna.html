Lasagna

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to make a scrumptuous lasagna.

This is an easy to follow recipe, that you will want to make many more times

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Sear the ground beef
  2. Add the tomato sauce
  3. put thin layer of ground beef in the dish
  4. Add a layer of bechamel sauce
  5. Add lasagna sheets
  6. Repeat until you have the desired amount of layers - Finish with a layer of ground beef and bechamel
  7. Add a layer of cheese on top of the Lasagna
  8. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 200C
  9. Let it rest for 15 minutes before serving
